Quick and easy Chicken Lunch

Ingredients

  • 250g chicken breast
  • 400g broccoli
  • 200g potatos

Preparations

Boil potatos
  1. Boil water and add potatos
  2. Boil potatos for 20-25m
Bake chicken breast
  1. Add oilive oil to pan and put fire on medium hot
  2. Add chicken and bake for 10-15m on medium hot fire
Bake broccoli
  1. Remove the chicken from the pan
  2. Add frozen broccoli and put fire on high
  3. Bake/stean broccoli with lid for 5-10m

Put everything on a plate and serve.
